Who We Are

One Foundation,
Countless Futures

Founded in 2015, InAmigos Foundation believes that every individual — child, woman, animal, or elder — deserves dignity, opportunity, and care. We unite volunteers, donors, and communities to create lasting change from the ground up.

🎓 Education 🍱 Food & Clothing 🐾 Animal Welfare 👩 Women Empowerment 🌿 Environment 🔧 Skill Development

"Alone we can do so little; together we can do so much — and InAmigos proves it every single day."

— Ritu Sharma, Founding Director
Our Initiatives

Six Projects, One Vision

Each initiative is a distinct commitment — rooted in a real need, driven by real people, and measured in real impact.

🎒 Education

Bachpanshala

Free learning centres bringing literacy, numeracy, and curiosity to underprivileged children aged 5–14, reaching those who fall between the gaps of formal schooling.

3,200+ Children enrolled
Support ›
🍱 Food & Clothing

Seva

Monthly food and clothing drives supplying nutritious meals to the hungry and warm garments to the homeless — because no one should face winter empty-handed.

18K+ Meals distributed
Support ›
🐾 Animal Welfare

Jeev

Rescue, rehabilitation, and adoption drives for street animals. Veterinary camps, sterilisation programmes, and community awareness build a more compassionate city.

900+ Animals rescued
Support ›
👩‍💼 Women Empowerment

Udaan

Empowering women through legal literacy, self-defence, entrepreneurship seed funding, and leadership workshops — giving every woman the wings she deserves.

1,400+ Women trained
Support ›
🌿 Environment

Prakriti

Tree plantation drives, river clean-ups, plastic-free campaigns, and eco-education in schools — restoring the balance between humanity and nature, one action at a time.

25K+ Trees planted
Support ›
🔧 Skill Development

Vikas

Vocational training in digital literacy, tailoring, carpentry, and more — equipping youth from marginalised communities with market-ready skills and livelihood pathways.

860+ Youth skilled
